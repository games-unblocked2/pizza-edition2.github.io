How to Play The Pizza Edition

  • Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to move your pizza delivery bike.
  • Avoid cars, potholes, and obstacles to keep your pizza intact.
  • Pick up pizza orders from the shop and deliver them to the right houses on time.
